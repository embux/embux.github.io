ICS-2010 Announcement - EMBUX Tiny Freescale Compact Box Computer

Posted on 2015-04-08   |   In events   |  

Embux Corporation launches its latest ICS-2010D series, a 2.5’’ Pico-ITX ARM-based embedded board with robust and rich I/O design. Embux RISC-based Single Board Computers (SBC) powered by Freescale ARM, provides multi-integrated functions for easy implementation. EA21-MX6 series meet the requirements for power and performance optimization, and help customers rapidly develop their own innovations in industrial control, home automation, medical device, HMI/KIOSK, digital signage, robotics and transportation segments.

Innovative Reliability Design

Embux ICS-2010D series are designed with all solid capacitors and 1% precision resistor, and all boards are also guaranteed with optional 100 times powered on/off testing before factory output. All these considerate steps are meant to make sure the best quality we may offer to our customers and provide the most trustable platform for customer diversified filed site application integration. In addition, industry and jump-less design could be quite suitable for numbers of rugged demand application as well.

Rich I/O Fulfill Diverse Integration & Application

With a wide variety of I/O interfaces including LVDS, HDMI, Gigabit LAN, USB, RS-232 (8-wire), RS-485, COM, and CAN-bus interface, ICS-2010D series could be implemented into diverse integration and application.

Multiple OS Support and Embedded Intelligent System (EiS)

Multiple operation system such as Linux and Android are supported on ICS-2010D. Moreover, in addition to OS services, an outstanding Embedded Intelligent System (EiS) is also provided by Embux. In real operation environments, engineers and operators may easily get ICS-2010D status information and do proper actions immediately. Remote monitoring becomes easy and convenient.

Main Features

  • Freescale Cortex-A9 i.MX6 DualLite 1.0 GHz
  • On-board DDR3 1GB
  • 1 x 18/24 bit LVDS output
  • 1 x HDMI 1920x1080
  • 1 x 10/100/1000 Mbps Ethernet
  • 3 x USB 2.0 (2 external + 1 internal)
  • 1 x RS-232 (8-wire), 1 x RS-485, 1 x CAN
  • 1 x mini-PCIe for WLAN/WWAN support
  • 1 x standard SD slot
  • 1 x EMBUX X-Port for EiS (EMBUX intelligent system)
  • Multiple OS support in Linux/Android
  • Low power consumption, 5V DC input
